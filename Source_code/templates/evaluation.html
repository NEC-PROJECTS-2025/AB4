Model Evaluation

The evaluation of our predictive models is based on precision, recall, F1-score, and accuracy. These metrics offer a detailed understanding of how well each model performs in classifying and predicting outcomes.

Model Accuracy Precision Recall F1-Score
Logistic Regression 93.35% 93% 92% 92%
SVM 94.48% 94% 93% 93%
KNN 90.65% 89% 89% 89%
Random Forest 99.44% 99% 99% 99%
XGBoost 99.47% 99% 99% 99%

The table above demonstrates that Random Forest and XGBoost exhibit superior performance with nearly perfect metrics, while Logistic Regression, SVM, and KNN also perform well, showcasing their ability to effectively classify and predict outcomes. These results reinforce the robustness of the models used in this project.
